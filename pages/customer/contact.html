Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse restaurants, select your favorite items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can track your order in real-time!

What's the minimum order value?

Minimum order value is $10. However, some restaurants may have their own minimum order requirements which will be displayed on their menu.

How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 30 minutes. However, delivery times may vary depending on restaurant location and current demand. You can see the estimated delivery time before placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (where available). All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before the restaurant starts preparing it. Once preparation begins, cancellation may not be possible. Contact our support team for assistance.

How do I become a partner restaurant?

Interested in partnering with us? Visit our "Partner With Us" page and fill out the registration form. Our team will review your application and get back to you soon!
