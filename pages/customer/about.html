About Us

Our Story

TasteNow was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make it easier for people to discover and order delicious food from local restaurants in their area. What started as a small project has grown into a platform serving thousands of customers daily.

We believe that local restaurants are the backbone of our communities, and we're dedicated to supporting them by bringing their delicious food directly to customers' doors. Our platform connects passionate restaurant owners with hungry customers, creating a win-win for everyone.

Today, TasteNow partners with over 500+ restaurants and has delivered more than 100,000+ meals to satisfied customers. We're proud of our growth and committed to continuous improvement.
